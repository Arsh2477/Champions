Australian Technical & Management College (ATMC) is a nationally recognised Australian educational institution that delivers a variety of higher education, VET, ELICOS and Professional Year programs. ATMC is well-established with multiple city-based campuses in Australia, spread throughout Sydney and Melbourne. Our higher education programs are delivered in partnership with Federation University & University of The Sunshine Coast, comprising a range of undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in Business, Accounting, IT and Management. ATMC VET and Baxter Institute, as part of ATMC offer an assortment of vocational education and training courses across various fields including engineering, automotive, construction, bakery and more.
